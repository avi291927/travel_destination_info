We started this because we love to travel.

WanderWorld began as a side project between a few friends who kept sharing travel tips in group chats. We figured — why not make it something anyone can use?

Today, we're a growing community of travelers who believe the best recommendations come from people, not algorithms. Every destination on here has been visited, vetted, and loved by someone in our community.
